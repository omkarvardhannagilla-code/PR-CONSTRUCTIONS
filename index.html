FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does PR Constructions provide?
PR Constructions offers residential construction (houses, villas, apartments), gated community development, commercial projects, architectural design, and complete turnkey solutions from planning to handover.
Where are you located in Hyderabad?
Our office is at Prashanthi Hills, Road No. 7, Meerpet, Hyderabad – 500097. We serve the entire Hyderabad metropolitan area including Meerpet, Saroor Nagar, L.B. Nagar, and surrounding regions.
Do you handle both residential and commercial construction?
Yes, we handle both residential and commercial construction projects of all scales — from individual homes to large gated communities and commercial complexes.
Do you assist with government approvals?
Absolutely. We handle the complete GHMC/HMDA approval process, building permits, layout approvals, and all statutory documentation. Our dedicated team ensures a completely hassle-free compliance experience.
What quality standards do you follow?
We use only ISI-certified materials, employ certified structural engineers, and conduct stage-wise quality inspections at every phase. We also offer third-party quality audits for premium projects upon request.
Do you offer a post-construction warranty?
Yes. Every project comes with a 1-year structural warranty and a dedicated after-sales support team to address any post-construction concerns promptly.
Can I visit ongoing or completed project sites?
Absolutely — we actively encourage site visits. Contact us to schedule a free site visit. Our team will walk you through the project, discuss your requirements, and answer all your questions over chai!
