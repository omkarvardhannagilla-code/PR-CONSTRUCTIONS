Our Story

Founded on Trust.
Built on Quality.

PR Constructions was founded in 2018 with a singular vision: to build homes that families are proud of, communities that people cherish, and projects that stand the test of time.

Starting with a small, dedicated team in Meerpet, Hyderabad, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted construction companies — with over 50 projects delivered and 300+ happy families across our developments.

Our philosophy is simple: every brick we lay carries our name. That's why we never compromise on materials, workmanship, timelines, or transparency — ever.
